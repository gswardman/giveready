# Onboard Your Nonprofit

GiveReady gives your charity a free donation page, a verified profile, and visibility to AI agents worldwide. No fees, ever.

## What You Get

**A donation page.** A clean, mobile-friendly page at `giveready.org/donate/your-charity-name` where anyone can send USDC directly to your wallet. Share the link anywhere — social media, your website, email campaigns.

**AI agent discovery.** Your charity appears in GiveReady's structured API, MCP server, and agent discovery files. When someone asks an AI assistant "find me a youth charity in the UK," your organisation shows up with verified data.

**Structured profile.** Your mission, programmes, impact metrics, and registration details in a machine-readable format that agents can parse, compare, and recommend.

## How to Sign Up

### Step 1: Fill in Your Details

Go to [giveready.org/onboard](https://giveready.org/onboard) and complete the 3-step wizard:

1. **About your charity** — name, mission, programmes, registration number, cause areas
2. **Set up your wallet** — connect a Squads multi-sig (recommended) or a Phantom wallet
3. **Preview and submit** — review your profile and donation page before going live

### Step 2: Verification

After you submit, GiveReady verifies your registration against official sources (UK Charity Commission, etc.). UK charities can be verified automatically using your registration number.

### Step 3: Go Live

Once verified, your donation page and API profile are immediately available. AI agents can find you through the MCP server and API.

## Requirements

- A registered charity or nonprofit entity
- A contact email
- A Solana wallet for receiving USDC donations (we help you set this up)

## Time to Complete

Most charities complete onboarding in under 10 minutes. Verification is typically same-day for UK registered charities.
