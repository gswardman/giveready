# Claim Your Nonprofit

GiveReady gives your nonprofit a free donation page, a verified profile, and visibility to AI agents worldwide. No fees, ever.

## What You Get

**A donation page.** A clean, mobile-friendly page at `giveready.org/donate/your-nonprofit-name` where anyone can send USDC directly to your wallet. Share the link anywhere — social media, your website, email campaigns.

**AI agent discovery.** Your nonprofit appears in GiveReady's structured API, MCP server, and agent discovery files. When someone asks an AI assistant "find me a nonprofit working on mental health," your organisation shows up with verified data.

**Structured profile.** Your mission, programmes, impact metrics, and registration details in a machine-readable format that agents can parse, compare, and recommend.

## How to Get Started

### Step 1: Find and Claim Your Page

Go to [giveready.org/onboard](https://giveready.org/onboard) and search for your nonprofit. With 41,000+ nonprofits already in the directory, you'll likely find your page already created. Claim it with your details.

If your nonprofit isn't listed yet, you can create a new profile with:
- Name, mission, programmes, registration number, cause areas

### Step 2: Set Up Your Wallet

Connect a Squads multi-sig (recommended for charities with trustees) or a Phantom wallet to receive donations.

### Step 3: Go Live

Once verified, your donation page and API profile are immediately available. AI agents can find you through the MCP server and API.

## Requirements

- A registered nonprofit entity
- A contact email
- A Solana wallet for receiving USDC donations (we help you set this up)

## Time to Complete

Most nonprofits complete onboarding in under 10 minutes if claiming an existing page, or 15 minutes for new profiles. Verification is typically same-day for registered charities.
