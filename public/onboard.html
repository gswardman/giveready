Get your nonprofit listed on GiveReady

Under 45 minutes. AI agents start finding you immediately. No fees, no intermediary, you keep 100% of every donation.

The process

Four steps. One afternoon.

You don't need a developer. You don't need crypto experience. Each step has a link to exactly what you need.

01
Set up a USDC wallet
Download Phantom (mobile or browser extension) or Coinbase Wallet. Create a wallet. Copy your Solana USDC address. No KYC required to receive funds. Works in any country.
~3 minutes
02
Register on GiveReady
Fill out the form below with your organisation name, mission, programmes, impact data, and wallet address. We verify your details and publish your profile. AI agents worldwide find you through our MCP server, API, llms.txt, and agents.md.
~10 minutes
03
Embed the donate button (optional)
Add two lines of HTML to your website. The widget reads your wallet address from GiveReady's API, generates Solana Pay QR codes and mobile wallet links. Donors scan, confirm, and USDC lands in your wallet. See the live demo.
<!-- GiveReady donate button --> <div id="giveready-donate" data-slug="your-org-slug"></div> <script src="https://giveready.org/widget/donate.js"></script>
~2 minutes
04
Set up a fiat off-ramp (optional)
If your nonprofit wants to auto-convert USDC to local currency: create an account on Coinbase (US, UK, EU), Luno (South Africa), or another local exchange. Link your bank account. USDC converts to fiat on arrival. This is the only step that requires KYC, and it's the exchange's KYC — not ours.
~30 minutes
What happens next

Once you're listed

✓ AI agents find you. When someone asks Claude, ChatGPT, or any MCP-connected assistant to find a youth charity, your nonprofit appears in the results with your mission, programmes, and impact data.
✓ Donors pay you directly. USDC goes straight to your wallet via x402 protocol. No intermediary touches the funds. No platform fees. You control the money from the moment it arrives.
✓ Your profile stays updated. Email us any time to update your programmes, impact metrics, or wallet address. We keep your data current so AI agents always have the latest.
✓ It stays free. No monthly fees. No transaction fees. No contract. GiveReady is open source and MIT licensed. If you ever want to leave, your wallet is yours — nothing to migrate.
