Paste this anywhere in your website's HTML. The widget loads automatically.

How it works

The script fetches your nonprofit's USDC wallet address from the GiveReady API and renders a donate button. When a donor clicks it, they choose an amount and get a Solana Pay QR code or a direct link to Phantom or Coinbase Wallet. The donation goes straight to your wallet. No backend required on your end. No fees.

The widget is 15KB, has zero dependencies, and works on any website — WordPress, Squarespace, Ghost, static HTML, anything that lets you paste a script tag.
