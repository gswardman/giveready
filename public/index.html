The problem

Every donation platform is a middleman

Every.org, The Giving Block, Endaoment — they hold your donations, convert them, and take a cut. None of them make your nonprofit visible to AI agents.

Donors are asking AI assistants to find causes, compare impact, and send money. If your nonprofit isn't in the answer, you're invisible to the fastest-growing donor channel on the planet.

How it works

Three steps. Under 45 minutes.

01
Set up a USDC wallet
Download Phantom or Coinbase Wallet. Create a wallet. Copy your USDC address. Three minutes, no KYC. Works in any country.
02
Register on GiveReady
Name, mission, programmes, impact data, wallet address. Ten minutes. AI agents worldwide find you through our MCP server, llms.txt, and API.
03
Embed the donate button
One script tag. Paste into your website. Generates Solana Pay QR codes, links to Phantom and Coinbase Wallet, sends USDC straight to your wallet.
